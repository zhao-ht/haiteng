Bio

I am a third year Ph.D student at the department of Machine Intelligence at Peking University. I am now doing research at Sigma Lab, Peking University, supervised by Prof.Zhihong Deng. I am also working at the University of Hong Kong with Lingpeng Kong and Qi Liu on many exciting projects. Previously, I received a B.Sc. degree in Psychology and I now aim to discover whether deep learning can encode human-like intelligence, reasoning and adaptation abilities, etc.

As a researcher, I am focused on exploring various aspects related to the generalization of deep models. This involves investigating domains such as domain adaptation, transfer learning, and robustness, among several other fascinating questions. Furthermore, I am particularly interested in investigating how deep models can effectively handle structured problems, which include understanding structured data and knowledge, structured and symbolic problem-solving, compositional generalization, and much more. I would be delighted for your chatting or collaboration!
